The Story of Marcella

Named after Chef Jose Sanchez's beloved grandmother, Marcella Restaurante is a tribute to the woman who ignited his passion for cooking. Her favorite flower, the violet orchid, symbolizes the blend of beauty, elegance, and creativity that defines our culinary experience.

Since 2009, Chef Sanchez has been on a culinary adventure, crafting a unique dining experience that honors local Puerto Rican ingredients while embracing international techniques. Each dish tells a story of respect for the land and its producers.

Our menu is a creative reinterpretation of Creole gastronomy—seasonal, locally sourced, and crafted in collaboration with island farmers. It's more than food; it's a celebration of heritage, artistry, and the warmth of Puerto Rican hospitality.
